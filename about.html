THE ZERO POINT & THE BREADCRUMBS

I didn't become "The Architect" because I wanted a career change. I became one because the system crashed and forced a hard restart.

In March 2024, I hit the Zero Point. You know that meme of the dog sitting in a burning room saying "This is fine"? I lived that. Except instead of sitting there, I opened the door, saw the fire, said "Nope," and walked out into the void.

Fast forward to a bridge in the middle of the city. I was watching the sunrise, ready to throw in the towel. But the universe intervened.

It wasn't a profound moment with angels playing harps. It was a glitch. A breadcrumb. Just enough distraction to break the loop. Those breadcrumbs—a sunrise that shouldn't have been there, heart-shaped rocks in the dirt—led my body away from the edge when my soul was too heavy to carry itself.

I realized I wasn't just "unlucky." I was being stripped. The old code—the people-pleasing, the frantic managing, the trying to be a "House" for everyone else—was deleted so I could understand my true function.

I am not the House. I am the Bridge.

My purpose isn't to shelter you; it's to help you cross over. To help business owners cross from chaos to flow. To help survivors cross from "survival mode" to "sovereignty."

The Ninth Wave is the methodology I built to survive the wipe. It combines the tactical precision of Ops Management with the reality-hacking principles of quantum mechanics.

It is the manual for finding and becoming the home you always longed for within yourself.
